Dorsen was founded on the observation that most mid-sized companies face the same problem: they need professional IT infrastructure but lack an internal team or budget for enterprise solutions.

Our approach is straightforward: we implement and maintain robust technology using open source and self-hosted solutions, eliminating unnecessary license fees and vendor lock-in.

Every project is documented, delivered with training and maintained with ongoing support. The client understands what was done and why.
